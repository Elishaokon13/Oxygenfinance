Why Oxygen?

Oxygen Finance was born out of a deep-rooted belief in the transformative power of blockchain technology and its potential to reshape the financial landscape for the better. The project's inception can be traced back to a defining moment in my life when

I witnessed the harsh realities of financial exclusion and inequality. I was introduced to the world of cryptocurrencies and blockchain technology during a period of global financial instability.

It became increasingly clear that traditional financial systems were riddled with disparities, inefficiencies, and barriers that left millions of people on the fringes of economic opportunity. This stark reality ignited the idea behind Oxygen Finance.
